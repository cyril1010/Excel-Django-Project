About Us

Located in the Kayalakathu Building, opposite the Church of God in Pravinkoodu, Chengannur, Mazhukeer, Kerala, Excel Sanitary and Plumbing Works is a trusted destination for all your sanitary and plumbing needs. With a stellar 5.0 rating from 22 reviews, the store has earned a reputation for excellent customer service and a wide range of quality products.
